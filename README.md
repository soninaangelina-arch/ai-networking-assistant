# AI Networking Assistant

This is an early-stage AI product concept focused on helping people discover relevant AI, tech and business events, prepare for networking conversations, and generate personalized outreach messages.

## What it does

The assistant is designed to help users:

- find relevant events based on their goals and interests
- prepare short personal introductions
- generate conversation starters for specific events
- organize useful contacts after networking
- create follow-up messages after meeting people

## Current focus

I am currently experimenting with prompt engineering and simple product workflows to make AI-generated recommendations more specific, practical and less generic.

One challenge I worked on was improving the structure of prompts so the assistant can produce better event recommendations and more personalized conversation openers based on the user's context, goals and industry.

## Technical challenge

A recent technical challenge was making the AI output feel more useful and personalized instead of generic. I tested different prompt formats, added more context about the user’s goals, and compared the quality of the generated recommendations and conversation openers.
